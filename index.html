Our Story

Master Craftsman's Legacy

For over 15 years, our workshop in Sukoharjo has been the birthplace of Indonesia's finest handcrafted gift packaging. What started as a passion project has evolved into a trusted name among discerning clients who understand that presentation is as important as the gift itself.

Every box that leaves our workshop carries the essence of traditional Indonesian craftsmanship, enhanced by modern design principles and an unwavering commitment to excellence. We don't just make boxes; we create experiences.
